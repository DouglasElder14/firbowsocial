What We Do

Our services.

Pick what you need. Leave the rest. Every service is available standalone or as part of a package.

Channel Strategy
A bespoke plan covering your target audience, the right platforms, and the metrics that actually matter to your business — not vanity numbers.
Social Media Reporting
Monthly, quarterly or annual reports with 5 metrics that matter to you. Or we build you a real-time dashboard so you always know what's happening.
Paid Social Strategy
Reach the right audience at the right time. We set up and run paid campaigns, or train you to run them yourself — your call.
Content Creation
Full content calendars including imagery and copy for 20+ posts. Images as standard; video available. We also build Canva templates so you can create content yourself.
Competitor Analysis
We analyse up to 3 of your competitors — what they post, what works, and what people are saying about them online.
Social Media Training
A half-day session with everything you need to manage your own social confidently. Plus free access to our Facebook community Q&A — ask us anything, anytime.
